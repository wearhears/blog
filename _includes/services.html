What is hEARS?



hEARS or the hEaring Aid Remodeled Smartly is a first-of-its-kind technology in healthcare today. With this simple but robust technology, hEARS answers the most common problems that hound elderlies –- hearing issues and poor accessibility to public and health services.


hEARS’ participation in the 2013 Young Innovators Challenge of the United Nation's International Telecommunication Union (ITU) has paved way for the team to be included in the organization’s List of 150 Innovators in 2015.
